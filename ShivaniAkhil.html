Our Story

We originally met through a modern fashion dating website which sparked countless conversations and built up to our first long distance date. It was at a coffee shop named Moxxee in West Virginia, and they serve the best lattes! Akhil later impressed Shivani on our second date, also in West Virginia, where we went on a private boat ride. We had a romantic lunch together on the boat, that Akhil had packed and so continued our long distance relationship up until our first weekend trip together to Florida where we both felt a bond and started liking each other even more. Thus, began our love story. After discovering our mutual love for traveling, adventure and a shared sense of humor, Akhil surprised Shivani with a perfect proposal in Texas in February 2017. With the support of family and friends, Shivani said ‘YES’!! Since then, we have traveled and been on many adventures together, fought and cried, but laughed harder and smiled bigger and have shared loads of incredible memories that we will cherish forever. We are very excited to celebrate the next chapter of our life with family and friends that we hold so dear to our hearts.
