Our Story

We both went to the same college for bachelors but our paths had never crossed in INDIA. Our story began when a mutual friend Jaynesh from bachelors introduced us. First, we met in 2015 in Texas, USA and gradually became best friends. We used to share a good Chemistry with each other. However our friendship fell apart when Ankur moved away from Texas. But destiny had something else in mind for us and we bonded again when Ankur came to visit a friend in NY. Neither of us have imagined that such a routine phone call would be the start of a fruitful and loving relationship. Finally, Ankur proposed Palak on 3rd Feb 2018 in Washington D.C. And now, we are so excited to celebrate the beginning of our new journey with family and friends.
