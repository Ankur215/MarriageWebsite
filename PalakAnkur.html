Our Story

Palak & Ankur both went to the same college for bachelors but their paths had never crossed in INDIA. Finally they met in 2015 in Texas, USA. Their story began in Texas, USA when a mutual friend Jaynesh from bachelors introduced them and gradually they became best friends. They used to have good Chemistry with each other. However their friendship fell apart when Ankur moved away from Texas. But destiny had something else in mind for them and they bonded again when Ankur came to visit a common friend in NY in dec 2017. Neither could have imagined that such a routine phone call would be the start of a fruitful and loving relationship. Ankur then proposed Palak on 8th Feb 2018 in Washington D.C. They are so excited to celebrate the beginning of their new journey with their family and friends.
